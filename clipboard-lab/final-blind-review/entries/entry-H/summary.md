This native macOS application is the most intentionally narrow entry in the set: a text-centric clipboard history tool built for quick cycling through recent snippets with as little ceremony as possible. Its design philosophy is conservative and developer-leaning. Rather than aiming for media-rich previews or workflow breadth, it keeps a remembered stack, a separate favorites store, a basic search box in the menu, and optional iCloud sync for users who want settings or clippings to travel. Security defaults are stronger than many older utilities because concealed, transient, and common password-manager types are skipped, but storage remains preference-based and no encryption-at-rest story was documented. The overall experience is easy to understand and likely lightweight in ordinary use, yet clearly limited on images, modern organization, and visual ambition compared with the more expansive entries.
